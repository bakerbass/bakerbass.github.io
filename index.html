About Me

Ryan Baker is an avid multi-instrumentalist, technologist and researcher. He is a graduate of the Frost School of Music at the University of Miami and is earning his Master's of Music Technology at Georgia Tech. Through his schooling, Ryan has found a passion for engineering that has fueled his ambitions as a creative technologist. Ryan's main research project involves teaching a robot how to play guitar in an audio-driven way.
