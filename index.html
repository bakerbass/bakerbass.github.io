About Me

Ryan Baker is an avid musician with skills on electric bass, upright bass, guitar, mandolin, banjo, and lap steel. He is a graduate of the Frost School of Music at the University of Miami. Through the Music Engineering program, Ryan has found a passion for engineering that has fueled his ambitions in software and electrical engineering. Ryan is focused on continuing his studies at Georgia Tech to further his research in music technology with a particular interest in human-computer interaction.
