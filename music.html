Pulp Love

An indie psychedelic-funk band based in Miami, FL.

Pulp Love started in 2021 to bring the worlds of psychedelic rock, jazz and new age funk together at the University of Miami. The 5-piece band records almost every show they play to be preserved as live albums, because no two shows are alike.

In the summer of 2023, the band went on tour up the eastern seaboard, performing shows in cities such as Orlando, FL, Atlanta, GA, Asheville, NC, and New York, NY.
